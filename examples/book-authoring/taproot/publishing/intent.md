# Intent: Publishing

## Goal
Enable the manuscript to be submitted to publishers or distributed to readers in the required format and with all necessary supporting materials.

## Stakeholders
- **Author**: needs a clear process for preparing final files and submission materials
- **Publisher / distributor**: needs files in the correct format with complete metadata
- **Reader**: needs a polished, correctly formatted final product

## Success Criteria
- [ ] All chapters are in `approved` state before submission preparation begins
- [ ] The manuscript is exportable in at least one required format (PDF, DOCX, EPUB)
- [ ] Submission materials (query letter, synopsis, author bio) are versioned alongside the manuscript
