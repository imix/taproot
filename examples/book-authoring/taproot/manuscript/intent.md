# Intent: Manuscript

## Goal
Enable the author to produce a well-structured, complete manuscript by organising content into chapters and maintaining consistent quality through review.

## Stakeholders
- **Author**: needs a clear structure for writing and revising chapters, and a way to track progress across the manuscript
- **Editor**: needs visibility into draft status and a consistent review process for each chapter
- **Reader**: needs a coherent, well-edited final text that flows from chapter to chapter

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Each chapter exists as a discrete unit that can be written, reviewed, and revised independently
- [ ] The status of every chapter (draft, in-review, revised, final) is visible at a glance in the hierarchy
- [ ] An editorial review process catches structural and quality issues before a chapter is finalised
