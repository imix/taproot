# Glossary

Project-wide term definitions. All specs must use these terms consistently.

## Manuscript

The full working draft of the book, including all chapters in current state. "Manuscript"
refers to the in-progress document — not a submitted or published version.

## Chapter

A discrete, self-contained section of the manuscript with its own argument or narrative arc.
Chapters are reviewed and approved independently. A chapter is not "done" until it has passed
editorial review.

## Draft vs Final

- **Draft** — a chapter or section open to structural and content changes
- **Final** — approved by the editor; only copyediting changes are permitted

Specs must not use "complete" to mean "final" — a complete draft may still require revision.
